Subject : Phishing Simulation Results - Q1 2024

All,

I wanted to share the results of our most recent phishing simulation campaign conducted on February 15-16, 2024.

CAMPAIGN SUMMARY:
- Total emails sent: 2,847
- Emails opened: 2,134 (74.9%)
- Links clicked: 412 (14.5%)
- Credentials submitted: 89 (3.1%)
- Reports to IT Security: 623 (21.9%)

While our click rate has improved from 22.3% in Q3 2023, the credential submission rate remains concerning. 89 employees entered their actual credentials on the simulated phishing page. This is particularly alarming given the recent actual security breach.

DEPARTMENT BREAKDOWN:
- Finance: 18.2% click rate (HIGHEST — mandatory retraining required)
- Sales: 16.7% click rate
- Marketing: 15.1% click rate
- Engineering: 8.4% click rate (LOWEST)
- Legal: 11.2% click rate

ACTION ITEMS:
1. All employees who submitted credentials will undergo mandatory security awareness training by March 15
2. Finance department will receive enhanced phishing training given their high click rate and the recent compromised accounts
3. We are implementing a new "Report Phishing" button in Outlook to make reporting easier
4. Starting Q2, employees who fail two consecutive phishing simulations will have their email access restricted to web-only (no mobile sync)

The next simulation is scheduled for Q2 2024. Please review the attached security awareness materials.
