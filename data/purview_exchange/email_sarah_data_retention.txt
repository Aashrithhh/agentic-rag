Subject : Updated Data Retention Policy for Exchange Archives

Team,

Following the Board's approval of the updated Information Governance Framework, I am issuing new data retention guidelines for all Purview Exchange email archives effective February 1, 2024.

KEY CHANGES:

1. RETENTION PERIODS:
   - Executive communications (C-suite): 10 years (increased from 7)
   - Financial/accounting emails: 7 years (SOX requirement)
   - Legal hold emails: Indefinite until hold is released
   - General business correspondence: 3 years
   - Marketing/promotional: 1 year
   - Personal/non-business: 90 days

2. LITIGATION HOLD PROCEDURES:
   - All litigation hold notices must be processed within 4 hours of receipt
   - IT must preserve ALL mailbox data for custodians named in a hold
   - Journaling must be enabled for all custodian mailboxes immediately
   - No PST files may be deleted while a litigation hold is active

3. PST EXPORT CONTROLS:
   - PST exports require manager + compliance approval
   - All PST files must be encrypted with AES-256
   - PST files must be logged in the chain-of-custody system
   - External transfer of PST files requires Legal sign-off

4. PURVIEW COMPLIANCE INTEGRATION:
   - We are migrating all Exchange compliance features to Microsoft Purview
   - eDiscovery searches will now be conducted through Purview Compliance Center
   - All sensitivity labels must be applied per the updated classification guide

Please ensure your teams are briefed on these changes before the February 1 effective date. Non-compliance will be treated as a policy violation subject to disciplinary action.
