INTERNAL MEMO
Date: 2024-03-15
From: Sarah Johnson, VP of Operations
To: All Department Heads

Subject: Q1 2024 Compliance Review

Please be advised that the quarterly compliance review has identified
several areas requiring immediate attention:

1. Environmental Reporting: Three facilities failed to submit monthly
   emissions data on time. This is a violation of EPA Form R requirements.

2. Worker Safety: Incident reports from the Baytown plant show a 15%
   increase in reportable injuries. OSHA inspections are expected.

3. Financial Controls: The internal audit team discovered unauthorized
   vendor payments totaling $45,000 in the Houston office.

All department heads must submit corrective action plans by March 30.
